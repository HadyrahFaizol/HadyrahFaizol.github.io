Brief Summary

I spent 7 years studying at primary school of Sekolah Kebangsaan Seri Sitiawan, and 5 years at secondary school of Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Seri Samudera. The feeling of going to school is a blend of anticipation, excitement, and a touch of nervousness. As the morning sun rises, a sense of purpose and adventure fills the air. There is an eagerness to reunite with friends, to engage in intellectual pursuits, and to embrace the opportunities that lie ahead. But amidst the excitement, there may also be a touch of nervousness. New challenges, assessments, and social dynamics may bring a hint of uncertainty. However, it is within these moments that resilience and personal growth find their roots. In the end, the feeling of going to school is a reminder of the transformative power of education. It is an invitation to embrace the joy of learning, to forge lasting connections, and to embark on a journey that shapes the very essence of who I am.
